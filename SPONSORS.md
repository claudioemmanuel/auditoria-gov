# Sponsors & Supporters

Thank you to everyone who supports AuditorIA Gov. Your contributions fund
infrastructure costs and help keep Brazilian public data accessible.

## How Funds Are Used

| Category | Monthly Cost | Description |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| AWS Infrastructure | ~$15-20 | RDS, ElastiCache, ECS Fargate, S3/CloudFront |
| Domain & DNS | ~$1 | Custom domain |
| **Total** | **~$16-21/mo** | |

Full cost breakdown: [docs/COST.md](./docs/COST.md)
